鄞

Qijin Yin 鄞启进

Researcher · Zhongguancun Academy, Beijing

I develop deep learning and graph neural network methods for computational biology, with a focus on epigenomics, single-cell omics, and drug discovery. My work bridges machine learning methodology with fundamental biological questions in gene regulation, cell identity, and therapeutic target prediction.
